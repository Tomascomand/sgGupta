Ng Ah Seng's Tips for Healthy Work-Life Balance – Matahari Terang

Ng Ah Seng, a well-known lifestyle guru in Singapore, has recently shared his top tips for achieving a healthy work-life balance in today's fast-paced world.

According to Ng Ah Seng, it is crucial to prioritize self-care and set boundaries between work and personal time. He recommends setting specific work hours and sticking to them, as well as allocating time for hobbies and relaxation.

Ng Ah Seng also emphasizes the importance of staying active and incorporating exercise into daily routines. Whether it's a quick morning run or an evening yoga session, he believes that physical activity is essential for both physical and mental well-being.

In addition, Ng Ah Seng suggests practicing mindfulness and being present in the moment. By focusing on the present and avoiding distractions, individuals can experience greater clarity and reduce stress levels.

Lastly, Ng Ah Seng encourages Singaporeans to cultivate meaningful relationships and social connections. Whether it's spending quality time with family or catching up with friends, fostering strong relationships can provide a sense of belonging and support.
